## TACTICAL (TAC) MODE (5.1.0)

A unit must be in Tac mode to:  
a) Breakdown or recombine (18.0).  
b) Be designated CR (5.8.0).  
c) Recover fatigue level (14.3.0).  
d) Conduct ferry operations (Eng units only) (17.2.0). Eng units in PA can ferry leg units (17.2.1).  
e) Perform any kind of construction (17.3.0).  
f) Receive RePs (22.5.0). Units in MR can receive RePs.  
g) Enter the map as reinforcements (21.0).

### Tactical Assault (TA) Designation (5.1.2)

Players may mark units TA in Attack Designation Segment (10.2.0). Units transported by TP or Allied inherent transport cannot be attack designated (7.12.3).

## PREPARED ASSAULT MODE (5.2.0)

The following units may not enter PA mode:  
a) HQs.  
b) Art units.  
c) Overstacked units (6.7.0).  
d) OoS units (15.6.0).  
e) Fatigue-2 units (14.2.2).  
f) OoC units (9.8.0).  
g) Units that could not conduct PA movement to reach a hex that is adjacent to an enemy unit.  
h) Units transported by TPs or Allied inherent transport (7.12.3a & b).  
**Important:** Players may not place units in PA mode where such placement would force the player to conduct more GAs than allowed by 16.3.3.

## EXPLOITATION MODE (5.3.0)

Units entering Exploit mode:  
a) Must place formation/sub-formation or single Recon unit in Exploit.  
b) Cannot be fatigue-1 or 2; and,  
c) Cannot be adjacent to an enemy unit; and,  
d) Must be in GenS and in command; and,  
e) Cannot be in a no fuel state.  
f) Must be a Mech class unit or a Leg class unit motorized using TP or Allied inherent transport.

## MANEUVER RESERVE MODE (5.4.0)

To enter MR mode:  
a) Have been allocated enough fuel points to be in a normal fuel status (16.4.2).  
b) Be in GenS.  
c) Be in command.  
d) Be within six hexes of its formation HQ.  
e) Be at least four hexes away from an enemy unit.

**MR Bonus Period (5.4.4):** Must have been in MR for 9 GTs. Bonus period is 6 GTs.  
**MR Bonuses:**  
a) Roll on the Maneuver Reserve MA Bonus Table.  
b) Favorable shift when participating in GA.  
c) Units automatically in GenS.  
d) Not subject to fatigue during ENA and rest GTs.

## STRATEGIC MODE (5.5.0)

Units in Strat mode increase MA by 50%. To enter Strat mode a unit must:  
a) Be Mech class unit or a Leg class unit transported by TP or Allied inherent transport.  
b) In GenS & in command.  
c) Eligible to conduct Mech road movement (7.10.0).  
d) Art units must be OoB.

## ARTILLERY UNITS & STANCE (5.6.0)

Players change Art units from IB to OoB, or from OoB to IB during the friendly Mode Determination Phase.  
**In Battery Towed Art Movement (5.6.1)**  
**Horse-drawn Artillery (5.6.2)**

## HQS & MODE (5.7.0)

HQs normally in Tac. May enter MR, Strat & Exploit mode.

## COMBAT RESERVE (CR) (5.8.0)

A player may designate a unit as a CR if the unit is:  
a) A personnel type unit.  
b) In Tac mode.  
c) Not adjacent to an enemy unit.  
d) A Bn. sized unit with at least two steps remaining.

### THREE STEP HYBRID UNIT STEP LOSS ORDER: (22.6.0)

**1st Step Loss:** The player receives the recycle steps for the least expensive AFV shown on the counter. To return the unit to full strength expend the ReP cost of the least expensive AFV.  
**2nd Step Loss:** The player receives the recycle step for the type of Inf or Recon shown on the counter.

- **To return the unit to a 2-step unit** expend the ReP cost of the Inf or Recon shown on the counter.  
- **To return the unit to full strength** (in subsequent GT) expend the ReP cost of the least expensive AFV.  

**3rd Step Loss:** The player receives the recycle steps for the most expensive AFV shown on the counter.

- **To resurrect the unit** expend the appropriate resurrection Mech class ReP steps and the ReP cost of the most expensive AFV shown on the counter.  
- **To return the unit to a 2-step unit** (in subsequent GT) expend the ReP cost of the Inf or Recon shown on the counter.  
- **To return the unit to full strength** (in subsequent GT) expend the ReP cost of the least expensive AFV.

### TWO STEP HYBRID UNIT STEP LOSS ORDER & REPLACEMENTS:

**1st Step Loss:** The player receives the recycle steps for the Inf or Recon shown on the counter.  
- **To return the unit to full strength** expend the ReP cost for the type of AFV shown on the counter.  

**2nd Step Loss:** The player receives the recycle steps for the type of AFV shown on the counter.  
- **To resurrect the unit** expend the appropriate resurrection Mech class ReP steps and the ReP cost of the AFV shown on the counter.  
- **To return the unit to full strength** (in subsequent GT) expend the ReP cost of the Inf or Recon shown on the counter.
