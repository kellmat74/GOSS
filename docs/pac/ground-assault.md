## Armor/Bonus Determination (13.8.4)

Pure AFV without combined arms have AF of zero unless the other side is only pure Arm.  
Result of "0" or less: No bonus.

### Attacking Armor vs Defending AT

**If Att AF & Def AT equal:** Stop (no bonus).  
**If not equal:** Subtract the smaller number from the larger.  
**If Def AT is the larger:** Do not modify result for Terrain/DW.  
**If Att AF is larger:** Modify result by Terrain/DW.

### Attacking Armor vs Defending Armor

**If Att & Def AF equal:** Stop (no bonus).  
**If not equal:** Subtract the smaller number from the larger.  
**If Att armor in clear:** Def may use clear terrain.  
**If Att Arm not in clear or Def does not choose option:** Modify result by Terrain modifiers. Do not use DW.

### Attacking AT vs Defending Armor

**If Att AT is greater than or equal to Def AF:** Stop (no bonus).  
**If Att AT is less than Def AF:** Subtract Att AT from Def AF and use Terrain modifiers only. Do not use DW.
